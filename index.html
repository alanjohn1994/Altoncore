Process

How It Works

From camera pixel to actionable coaching cue in under 30 milliseconds.

  1. Capture

    A camera or phone records the exercise using any standard webcam, IP camera, or mobile device — no depth sensors or wearables needed.

  2. Analyse

    Our CV models estimate pose and detect deviations from safe form, measuring joint angles, symmetry, and movement velocity in real time.

  3. Coach

    Real‑time cues guide the athlete through each rep, followed by a post‑session report with corrective drills tailored to detected faults.
